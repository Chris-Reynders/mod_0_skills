**Condiment Class**


**Attribute**

  - flavor (string)
  - bottleSize (integer)
  - expireDate (datetime)
  - currentContents (float)
  - temperature (float)


**Method**  

  - waterDown (modifies flavor)
  - cut (modifies bottleSize)
  - tamper (modifies expireDate)
  - pourOut (modifies currentContents)
  - refrigerate (modifies temperature)
