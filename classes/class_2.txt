**Dessert Class**

  
**Attribute**

  - cold (boolean)
  - calories (int)
  - nutAllergen (boolean)
  - readyBy (datetime)
  - menuName (string)

**Method**

  - cook (modifies cold=FALSE)
  - whippedCream (modifies calories)
  - noNuts (modifies nutAllergen=FALSE)
  - fromScratch (modifies readyBy)
  - rename (modifies menuName)
